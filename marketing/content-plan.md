## Next Content Decisions

Create a dedicated "free resume checker" or "free CV checker" guide only after one of these is true:

- Search Console shows impressions for those generic queries but existing URLs have weak CTR.
- Social/community questions show users are asking for checker workflow guidance.
- The page can include unique examples, screenshots, or a comparison table that does not duplicate the homepage and score guide.

Do not publish thin variations such as "best resume checker," "online CV checker," and "ATS CV checker" unless each has a distinct search intent and unique content.
