## Outreach Sequence

1. Start with 10 low-risk partners: university career centers, bootcamps, career coaches, resume writers, and newsletters.
2. Share no-link educational content in communities for 1-2 weeks before linking.
3. Use directory submissions only where ApplyReadyCV is a genuine fit.
4. Track every outreach attempt with date, target, message, result, and next step.
5. Stop posting in any community where moderators remove or warn against the content.
